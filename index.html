Los Mexikas

Welcome to Bratislava! In the first authentic Mexican restaurants in Bratislava and in all of Slovakia, at "Los Mexikas", Chef Mauricio Aceves Omana, a passionate Mexican cook, takes you on a daily journey through the culinary delights of Mexico. Original Mexican dishes, such as tacos al pastor, barbacoa consommé, huaraches, enchiladas and also vegetarian variations, are prepared in front of you. Typical Mexican drinks complete the enjoyment. Eat and drink like in Mexico - ¡Buen Provecho!

Services

"A Culinary Journey to Mexico"


Description

In our Mexican restaurant, we strive to offer our clients an authentic and memorable experience that transports them to Mexico through the flavors, the aromas and colors of our kitchen. Our service theme "A Culinary Journey to Mexico" focuses on providing a unique experience that combines the authenticity of Mexican dishes with a cozy and authentic atmosphere that reflects Mexican culture.

Welcome

Our clients are greeted with a warm welcome and are offered a table that suits their needs.

Drink service

Our staff offers you a variety of typical Mexican drinks, such as margaritas, micheladas, tequila, mezcal and Mexican beers, to accompany your meal.

Selection of dishes

Our customers can explore our wide variety of typical Mexican dishes, including tacos, burritos, enchiladas, guacamole and salsas.

Preparing the dishes

Our cooks prepare the dishes in an authentic way, the dishes are served in clay dishes, using fresh and high quality ingredients to guarantee the best taste and quality.

Dessert

To end your culinary experience, we offer a variety of typical Mexican desserts.

Farewell

Our clients are greeted with a smile and the promise to visit us again soon.

With our service theme "A Culinary Journey to Mexico," we ensure that each client has an authentic and memorable experience that transports them to Mexico through the flavors and aromas of our kitchen. We hope you enjoy your culinary journey and come back soon to continue exploring the authentic Mexican food we offer.
